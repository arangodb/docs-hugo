The Agentic AI Suite turns your data into a contextual knowledge layer that
AI agents and copilots can reason over. It combines automated knowledge graph
construction, GraphRAG retrieval, graph machine learning, and natural language
interfaces, all running on top of ArangoDB and delivered through the Arango
Contextual Data Platform.

For the full list of components, where their data is stored, the LLM and
embedding models the suite supports, and how to load sample datasets, see the
[Overview](overview.md).
