The tutorials in this section follow fictional companies through complete
journeys on the Arango Contextual Data Platform. Each tutorial solves one
concrete pain point at a time and introduces one layer of the platform, so
that by the end you have both a working system and an understanding of why
each piece is there.

## Available tutorials

- [**End-to-End Tutorial**](end-to-end-tutorial/_index.md): Follow
  Nordweave, a fictional mid-to-premium clothing retailer, on its
  journey from a traditional PostgreSQL backend to a graph-native,
  AI-ready data platform. Starts from raw data import and ends with an
  agentic merchandising assistant grounded in catalog and review data.
